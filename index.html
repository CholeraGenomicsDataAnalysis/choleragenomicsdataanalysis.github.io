Collaborating across Africa to enhance genomic surveillance of Vibrio cholerae

A continent-wide initiative to co-develop pipelines, share expertise, and strengthen public health response through genomic insights.

Our Core Objectives

Platform Compilation

Review and integrate bioinformatics tools used for cholera genomics.

Identify Gaps

Highlight barriers to using genomic platforms in African settings.

Decision Support

Develop a guide to help users select appropriate genomic tools.

User-Friendly Guides

Create accessible SOPs and walkthroughs for using genomic pipelines.

Innovative Platform

Reimagine and propose a next-gen cholera genomics analysis platform.

Collaborative Network

Build a network of African scientists to share knowledge and resources.
